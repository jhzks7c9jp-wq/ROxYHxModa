Target Audience
Behaviour-based personas
01
City Switchers
Relocating to London. Need a base that just works instantly. Often moving for work — corporate relocation or new role. No admin, no setup. Move in Monday, operational by Tuesday.
02
Routine Optimisers
Managing fragmented bills and flatshare logistics. Want a home that handles life's background tasks so they can focus on what matters. All-inclusive is the proposition.
03
Community Seekers
Outgrown flat-shares but not ready to live alone. Want genuine social fabric — real connections, shared spaces that activate organically — not just a serviced apartment.

Execution
90-day pre-launch plan July → October PC
July
Learn the infrastructure
Moda SOPs end-to-end. Not just reading — interrogating. Where does each SOP assume a mature scheme? Where does it need adapting for a 209-unit co-living pre-launch?
Spike Global — beyond proficient. Build the full enquiry-to-move-in workflow. Understand how the system breaks when process isn't followed. Train-ready by end of month.
Internal stakeholder mapping. Meet marketing, asset management, and head office leasing contacts. Understand who owns which decisions before making any.
Competitor site visits. Walk every comparable in person — ARK Canary Wharf, The Collective, Television Centre. Written about them; now stand in them.
ERV baseline. Validate opening rents against live W12 market data. Establish the pricing model before it gets locked.
Waitlist infrastructure live. Pre-registration page active, CRM capturing and categorising leads by persona and source from day one.
August
Build the pipeline
Corporate partnership outreach. Imperial College, BBC Media Village, White City Incubator, Westfield corporate contacts. Long lead times — start now, convert at PC.
Marketing collaboration. Feed ground-level insight to the marketing team — prospect objections, qualifying questions, what the W12 renter actually asks on a viewing. Their content, sharper.
Third-party agent briefing. Align on commission structure, referral process, and brand standards. Controlled channel, not a parallel sales team.
Viewing script drafted and reviewed. Five core talking points. All-in cost comparison as the leave-behind. Ready for team training by September.
Waitlist nurture cadence active. Three-email sequence from sign-up to booked viewing. SLA: same-day response to every inbound enquiry.
Open House planning. Format confirmed, guest list pipeline identified, event brief to marketing. Community before first move-in.
September
Stress-test everything
Full process simulation. Run the enquiry-to-application journey end-to-end in Spike. Find the breaks before launch — not after first move-in.
Team recruited, trained, signed off. Leasing associates competent on SOPs, Spike workflow, viewing script, and RRA compliance. No gaps on day one.
Corporate pipeline review. Which relationships have progressed to confirmed interest? Which need a different approach before PC?
Final ERV sign-off. Opening rents sense-checked against live market moves since July. Incentive structure agreed with head office.
RRA compliance audit. Every piece of copy, every channel, every application template checked. Income verification workflow tested in full.
KPI dashboard live. Weekly reporting framework agreed with Moda. Waitlist→viewing, viewing→application, cost per lead all tracked from PC day one.
October — PC & Launch
Open House activations · Waitlist converts to signed tenancies · Community event week one · Target 40–50% occupancy within 30 days

Operations
Operational excellence
Enquiry management
Structured CRM. All leads categorised by persona and source. Same-day response standard. Every enquiry tracked from channel to close.
Revenue management
Monthly revenue review against occupancy pace. Incentive structure pre-agreed with head office. ERV reviewed at 90 days post-launch.
Viewings
Scripted around 5 core talking points. All-in cost comparison shown as a leave-behind on every visit. Closing at point of viewing, not follow-up.
Retention
Welcome pack on day one. Events calendar communicated at point of application. Renewal conversations begin at week eight, not week twelve.
Compliance
RRA-compliant copy in all materials. Income verification built into online applications. Periodic tenancy documentation ready for Renters Rights Act alignment.
Reporting
Weekly leasing dashboard for Moda head office. Cost per acquisition tracked from day one. Enquiry-to-viewing, viewing-to-offer conversion reported weekly.
